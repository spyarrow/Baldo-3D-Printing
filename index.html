Material Readiness

High-Performance Filaments

Every material runs on the Flashforge AD5X CoreXY platform at ±0.1mm tolerance.

🟡 Ready To Print

PLA+

Premium entry-level. Crisp detail, easy post-processing. Ideal for display props, cosplay pieces, and concept models.

  • Print Temp: 200–220°C
  • Great Surface Finish
  • Easy To Paint & Sand
🔵 Ready To Print

PETG

Flexible, chemical-resistant, and food-safe. Best for enclosures, wearable fixtures, and mechanical brackets.

  • Print Temp: 230–250°C
  • Impact-Resistant
  • Moisture-Resistant
🔴 Ready To Print

ABS

Heat-resistant engineering grade. Optimal for mechanical assemblies, automotive parts, and high-stress applications.

  • Print Temp: 230–250°C
  • High-Temp Tolerance
  • Acetone-Smoothable
⚫ Premium Material

CF-Nylon

Carbon-Fiber Nylon composite. Extreme stiffness-to-weight ratio for drone frames, structural jigs, and industrial parts.

  • Print Temp: 260–280°C
  • Superior Stiffness
  • Aerospace-Grade Strength
